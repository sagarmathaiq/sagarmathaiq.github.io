The Project

SagarmathaIQ started as a weekend exercise: could a probabilistic model - built on public data, zero-sum vote share distributions, and Monte Carlo simulation - forecast Nepal's 2082 parliamentary elections at the constituency level?

We are not political scientists or professional forecasters. We are researchers with backgrounds in data science, statistical modeling, and materials science who share an interest in Nepal and a belief that transparent, quantitative analysis is more useful than punditry. All methodology and results are published openly.
