Understanding Win Probabilities: The win probability shows how often each party wins the seat across simulations. In competitive 3-way or 4-way races, the most likely winner may have less than 50% probability - this is normal for FPTP (first-past-the-post) voting where you only need more votes than any other party, not a majority. Example: If UML wins 40% of simulations, NC wins 35%, and RSP wins 25%, UML is the "likely winner" even though they win less than half the time.
